Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's simple, yet rich and flavorful. Follow the instructions below to prepare this delicious meal:

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti : Use high-quality spaghetti for the best texture.
  • Pancetta : Diced pancetta adds a savory, salty flavor.
  • Eggs : Provides the creamy base for the sauce.
  • Pecorino Romano cheese : Grated cheese adds sharpness and richness.
  • Black pepper : Freshly ground black pepper gives the dish its distinctive spice.
  • Salt : Just a pinch for seasoning the pasta water.

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. In a separate pan, cook the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl and mix in the grated Pecorino Romano cheese.
  4. Drain the spaghetti, reserving some pasta water.
  5. Toss the hot spaghetti with the pancetta and its rendered fat.
  6. Remove the pan from heat, then slowly stir in the egg and cheese mixture.
  7. Add reserved pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  8. Season generously with freshly ground black pepper and serve immediately.
